What you need
Five things to get started

    A Claude subscription The AI that powers HELM. Pro plan (~$20/month) covers most users.
  • 2
    A machine you can leave on 24/7 A Mac Mini works great. So does an old laptop or any PC not in daily use. Dedicated is strongly recommended — scheduled tasks never miss.
  • 3
    A Discord account Free. Your command center for HELM. Works from phone, desktop, or web.
  • 4
    A password manager HELM uses it to store credentials securely. Most major managers are supported. It never sees your personal passwords.
  • 5
    About 60 minutes Setup is guided step by step. You don't need to prepare anything before you start.
Honest tradeoffs

Setup takes about an hour — guided the whole way. The first week is useful. The third month is noticeably better. After six months, it knows your patterns. HELM is a force multiplier, not a replacement for judgment. The decisions still belong to you.
